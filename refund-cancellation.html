Refund & Cancellation Policy

Last updated: March 15, 2024

1. Subscription Cancellation

You may cancel your subscription at any time:

  • Log in to your account
  • Navigate to subscription settings
  • Click on "Cancel Subscription"
  • Access continues until the current billing period ends

2. Refund Policy

Our refund policy includes:

  • 7-day refund window from subscription date
  • Full refund if service is unavailable
  • Partial refund for technical issues
  • No refund for unused subscription time

3. Refund Process

To request a refund:

  • Contact support within eligible period
  • Provide subscription details
  • State reason for refund request
  • Processing time: 5-7 business days

4. Non-Refundable Cases

Refunds will not be provided for:

  • Requests after 7 days of purchase
  • Violation of terms of service
  • Account sharing violations
  • Partial month usage

5. Auto-Renewal

Subscription Management:

  • Subscriptions auto-renew by default
  • Cancel auto-renewal anytime
  • Notification before renewal
  • Price changes notified in advance
